General settings are the first section in Project Settings.

## Project details

The General section shows basic identifying information such as the project name.

## Export

Export belongs in General settings, not the IDE toolbar.

The export action is currently disabled until the Mac infrastructure and archive flow are ready.

## Navigation

Open Project Settings from the workspace action menu in the IDE header.
